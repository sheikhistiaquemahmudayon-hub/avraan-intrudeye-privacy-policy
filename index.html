Privacy Policy for Avraan IntrudEye

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Avraan developed the IntrudEye application as a free security and anti-theft service. This service is provided by Avraan at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

IntrudEye may request permissions including Camera, Location, Accessibility Service, and Device Administrator permissions for anti-theft and security functionality.

1. Camera Access

IntrudEye may capture a photo when an unauthorized unlock attempt is detected.

2. Location Access

The app may access device location, including background location, to help users locate lost or stolen devices.

3. Device Administrator Permission

Device Administrator permission is used for security and unlock monitoring features.

4. Accessibility Service

Accessibility Service is used only for lock screen interaction and unauthorized access detection.

Firebase and Third-Party Services

The app may use Firebase services for notifications and security alerts.
