Privacy Policy for Avraan IntrudEye

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

Avraan IntrudEye ("the App") is a personal security and anti-theft application developed and maintained by Avraan. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information when you use the service.

Information Collection and Use

To provide anti-theft and device security features, the App may request certain permissions from your device. These permissions are used solely for the functionality described below.

Camera Permission

The App may capture a photo when an unauthorized unlock attempt is detected. Captured images are used only for security purposes.

Location Permission

The App may access device location, including background location, to help locate a lost or stolen device and provide security-related alerts.

Device Administrator Permission

Device Administrator permission may be required to support anti-theft, device protection, and unauthorized access monitoring features.

Accessibility Service Permission

Accessibility Service is used exclusively for security-related functionality, including unauthorized access detection and lock-screen monitoring. The service is not used to collect personal information, monitor user activity, or access sensitive data unrelated to the App's security features.

Data Storage

Security-related information generated by the App may be stored locally on the device or securely transmitted through Firebase services when necessary to provide notifications and security alerts.

Third-Party Services

The App may use trusted third-party services that have their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)

Data Security
